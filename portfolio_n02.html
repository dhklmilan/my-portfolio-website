NO2 Concentration in Kathmandu Valley

  • Date: 2023
  • GEE Script

NO2, as a major air pollutant and a component of smog. Analyzing NO2 concentrations over a one-year period (2023) of Kathmandu Valley, utilizing Sentinel-5P NRTI L3 Products, shows that nitrogen dioxide concentration is higher inside the ring road, denoted by red color. April 19 records the highest NO2 concentration (0.00012 ppm). The mean value of NO2 concentration over a year is 0.00007171 ppm. The primary reason for ambient air concentration inside the ring road is likely due to road traffic and industrial processes and utilities. What are the consequences of ambient NO2 concentration? Obviously, when NO2 is chemically transformed into nitric acid, it can corrode metals, increase AQI, damage trees and crops, resulting in substantial effects.
