Mapping the devastated flash flood in Melamchi

  • Date: 2021
  • GEE Script

The Melamchi River in Nepal faced severe flooding in June 2021. The disaster began with heavy precipitation in the upstream zones, setting off a series of hazards along the river paths, resulting in numerous casualties and significant damage to infrastructure.

The snapshot (red-colored area) illustrates the satellite-detected flash flood in Melamchi Municipality. While analyzing (Sentinel-1 SAR GRD C BAND SAR) data of Melamchi, around 16.63 square kilometers of area was affected between June 22-25, 2021. This is a preliminary analysis utilizing satellite imagery that has not been validated in the field.
